# Energy Contracts Dashboard

The Energy Contracts Dashboard is a full-stack web application that enables energy traders to browse a marketplace of energy contracts, build and manage a personal portfolio, and analyze price trends across energy types — all from a single, unified interface.

## What is the Energy Contracts Dashboard?

The platform connects energy buyers and sellers through a structured contract marketplace. Each contract represents a commitment to deliver a specific quantity of energy (measured in MWh) at a set price, from a defined location, over a delivery window.

Users can:

- **Browse contracts** filtered by energy type, price range, quantity, location, and delivery dates
- **Compare contracts** side-by-side (up to 3 at once) to make informed decisions
- **Build a portfolio** by adding contracts and tracking aggregate metrics
- **Sell contracts** to mark them as fulfilled and remove them from the active market
- **Analyze trends** with interactive charts showing average prices per energy type over time

## Energy types supported

The platform supports contracts across eight energy categories:

| Energy Type  | Description |
|---|---|
| **Solar**       | Photovoltaic and concentrated solar generation |
| **Wind**        | Onshore and offshore wind generation |
| **Nuclear**     | Baseload nuclear power generation |
| **Hydro**       | Hydroelectric power generation |
| **Geothermal**  | Geothermal heat and power generation |
| **Natural Gas** | Natural gas combustion generation |
| **Coal**        | Coal-fired thermal generation |
| **Oil**         | Oil-fired generation |

## Key concepts

**Contract** — The core data unit. Each contract has an energy type, quantity (MWh), price per MWh, delivery window (start and end dates), location, provider, and status (`Available` or `Sold`).

**Portfolio** — A user's personal collection of contracts. The portfolio view aggregates metrics like total capacity (MWh), total cost, and average price per MWh, with a breakdown by energy type.

**Price Trends** — Aggregated monthly average prices per energy type, visualized as line charts for market analysis.
